About Me


I'm Ryan, a web developer/designer with a background in business and a passion for code, design, and problem solving. When I graduated from San Francisco State University with a degree in Business Information Systems, I decided I wasn't done learning.


Building and tinkering websites is something I've always loved to do, but I had plenty of areas to improve on. This past year I decided to jump into the deep end to learn new tools and techniques through various courses and projects.


Some resources I've used to build on my skills include CodeSchool, Codecademy, r/webdev, Smashing Magazine, CSS-Tricks, Eloquent Javascript, and Javascript & JQuery. Check out some of my work below!
